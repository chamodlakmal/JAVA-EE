National Telecommunication Museum

                    SLT is an integral part of this proud history and continues to evolve with technology and industry advancements. In order to showcase and share this memorable and transformative journey with the entire nation, SLT decided to convert its iconic Padukka Satellite Station into a museum that will display SLT’s valuable collection of communications items and take its visitors on a transformative journey of Sri Lanka’s telecommunications industry.
